It All Started...

Boy parks car for interview at Ferrari. During interview, Girl leaves work at Ferrari and backs into Boy’s car in the parking lot.

Boy and Girl work at Ferrari for a couple of months.

Girl quits Ferrari and they lose touch for a while.

Girl’s apartment is near Boy’s bartending job in Boston. Girls walks by bar and notices "Norwood High School Mustang" bumper sticker on Boy’s Volvo.

Girl stops in bar to see Boy. Girl starts stopping by the Beacon Hill Pub for a quick chat and a drink with Boy.

Boy and Girl are both single at this time...

After several months of gym dates, dancing, music, and deep thoughts... Girl and Boy start dating!
